Cherrapunji – The Land of Living Root Bridges & Waterfalls

Just 55 km from Shillong, Cherrapunji (locally called Sohra) is famous for being one of the wettest places on Earth. This misty paradise is known for its stunning waterfalls, caves, and unique bridges made from living tree roots. The charm of this place lies in its lush green hills, cascading waterfalls, pristine lakes, and charming villages. Nature lovers will be captivated by its enchanting beauty, while trekkers and adventurers can explore numerous trails that lead to hidden gems.

Top Highlights to Visit in Cherrapunji:
  • Dawki: A thrilling drive through deep gorges to reach the crystal-clear Umngot River.
  • Nohkalikai Falls: India’s tallest plunge waterfall (1,115 feet).
  • Seven Sisters Falls: A breathtaking sight of seven parallel waterfalls.
  • Garden of Caves, Mawsmai Caves, Arwah Caves: Stunning limestone caves with unique rock formations.
  • Thangkharang Park: A viewpoint offering mesmerizing views of the Bangladesh plains.
  • Eco Park: A peaceful nature retreat with scenic beauty.
Unique Experiences in Cherrapunji:
  • Trek to the world-famous Double Decker Living Root Bridge in Nongriat.
  • Boating at the serene and transparent Umngot River in Dawki.
  • Experience thrilling adventure activities in Shnongpdeng.
  • Explore hidden wonders in Cherrapunji’s limestone caves.
  • Indulge in local Khasi cuisine, especially Pork with Bamboo Shoots.
Best Time to Visit:

September to May: Pleasant weather and lush green landscapes.
June to August (Monsoon): Witness Meghalaya’s waterfalls in full glory (prepare for heavy rain).

Cherrapunji Itinerary – 3 Days & 2 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Shillong & Transfer to Cherrapunji
  • Arrive at Shillong and drive towards Cherrapunji.
  • Visit Umiam Lake en route for a scenic stop.
  • Explore Garden of Caves and Mawsmai Caves.
  • Overnight stay in Cherrapunji.
Day 2: Cherrapunji Sightseeing & Living Root Bridge Trek
  • Start your day with a visit to Nohkalikai Falls, India’s tallest waterfall.
  • Visit Seven Sisters Falls and Thangkharang Park.
  • Embark on a trek to the iconic Double Decker Living Root Bridge in Nongriat.
  • Enjoy a traditional Khasi dinner.
  • Overnight stay in Cherrapunji.
Day 3: Dawki & Shnongpdeng Adventure – Return to Shillong
  • Drive to Dawki and enjoy a boat ride on the crystal-clear Umngot River.
  • Experience adventure activities in Shnongpdeng (zip-lining, kayaking, cliff jumping).
  • Return to Shillong and depart for your onward journey.
