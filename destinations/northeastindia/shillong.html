Shillong – The Scotland of the East

Shillong, the capital of Meghalaya, is a vibrant hill station known for its colonial charm, music culture, and natural beauty. Surrounded by pine-covered hills and sparkling lakes, Shillong is a favorite among nature lovers, couples, and adventure seekers.

Top Highlights to Visit in Shillong:
  • Umiam Lake: A serene lake perfect for boating and picnics
  • Shillong Peak: The highest viewpoint in the city offering panoramic views
  • Elephant Falls: A gushing three-tiered waterfall amidst the rich greenery
  • Don Bosco Museum: A cultural hub showcasing Northeast India’s rich heritage
  • Ward’s Lake: A picturesque lake ideal for leisurely walks
  • Mawphlang Sacred Forest: Known for its unique ecological importance and beauty
  • Police Bazar: Bustling with life, Police Bazar is the heart of Shillong’s shopping scene
  • Lady Hydari Park: A peaceful retreat with lush greenery
  • Laitlum Canyon: Stunning viewpoints of hills and valleys
  • Jowai & Krang Suri Falls: A perfect day excursion with lakes, falls, and scenic views
Things to Do in Shillong:
  • Enjoy a boat ride on Umiam Lake
  • Explore local markets like Police Bazaar for shopping
  • Experience Shillong’s vibrant music scene in local cafes and pubs
  • Try traditional Khasi cuisine like Jadoh (rice & meat) and Dohneiiong (pork curry)
Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit Meghalaya is from September to May when the weather is pleasant. If you love monsoon, visit between June and August to witness Meghalaya's breathtaking waterfalls.

Shillong Itinerary - 3 Days & 2 Nights

Day 1: Arrival & Local Exploration
  • Arrive in Shillong and check into your hotel.
  • Visit Umiam Lake for boating and scenic views.
  • Explore the bustling Police Bazaar for shopping and street food.
  • Enjoy a cozy dinner at a local Khasi restaurant.
Day 2: Shillong Sightseeing
  • Start your day at Shillong Peak for breathtaking panoramic views.
  • Visit the beautiful Elephant Falls.
  • Explore the Don Bosco Museum to learn about Northeast India's rich culture.
  • Spend your evening at Ward’s Lake or Laitlum Canyon.
Day 3: Excursion to Jowai & Krang Suri Falls
  • Take a short trip to Jowai to visit the stunning Krang Suri Falls.
  • Explore Thadlaskein Lake and the historic Nartiang Monoliths.
  • Return to Shillong and depart for your onward journey.
