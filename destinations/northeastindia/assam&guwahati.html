Assam – A Land of Wildlife, Culture & Adventure

Assam, the gateway to Northeast India, is a paradise for wildlife enthusiasts, adventure seekers, and culture lovers. Home to India’s best national parks and vibrant city life in Guwahati, Assam offers an unforgettable travel experience.

Top Tourist Destinations in Assam:
  • Kaziranga National Park: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, famous for the one-horned rhinoceros and diverse wildlife.
  • Pobitora Wildlife Sanctuary: Known as the "Mini Kaziranga," it has the highest density of one-horned rhinos.
  • Nameri National Park: Perfect for river rafting and jungle trekking amidst the Himalayan foothills.
  • Manas National Park: A UNESCO-listed site known for tigers, elephants, and golden langurs.
  • Guwahati: The cultural hub of Assam, home to the famous Kamakhya Temple and scenic Brahmaputra River cruises.
Best Things to Do in Assam:
  • Go on a thrilling Jeep & Elephant Safari at Kaziranga.
  • Enjoy river rafting at Nameri National Park.
  • Explore the vibrant markets & Assamese culture in Guwahati.
  • Take a breathtaking Brahmaputra River Cruise at sunset.
  • Visit the ancient Kamakhya Temple, one of India’s most revered Shakti Peethas.
Best Time to Visit:

November – April: Ideal for safaris, wildlife spotting, and cultural sightseeing.
October – May: Perfect time to explore Guwahati and Assam’s rich cultural heritage.

How to Reach Assam?
  • By Air: Nearest airport – Guwahati (well connected to major cities).
  • By Train: Nearest railway station – Guwahati Railway Station.
  • By Road: Assam is well connected via National Highways from major cities.

Assam Itinerary – 6 Days & 5 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Guwahati & Transfer to Kaziranga
  • Arrive in Guwahati and drive towards Kaziranga National Park.
  • Enjoy a relaxing evening at Kaziranga Orchid Park.
  • Overnight stay in Kaziranga.
Day 2: Kaziranga Safari & Transfer to Nameri
  • Early morning Elephant Safari to spot rhinos & tigers.
  • Afternoon Jeep Safari to explore deeper parts of the forest.
  • Drive to Nameri National Park and overnight stay.
Day 3: Nameri to Manas National Park
  • Enjoy river rafting on the Jia Bhoroli River.
  • Explore Nameri’s jungle trails with a guide.
  • Drive to Manas National Park and overnight stay.
Day 4: Manas Safari & Transfer to Pobitora
  • Morning Jeep Safari at Manas to spot tigers & golden langurs.
  • Drive to Pobitora Wildlife Sanctuary.
  • Overnight stay in Pobitora.
Day 5: Pobitora Safari & Transfer to Guwahati
  • Early morning Jeep Safari at Pobitora.
  • Drive to Guwahati and visit Kamakhya Temple.
  • Evening Brahmaputra River Cruise.
  • Overnight stay in Guwahati.
Day 6: Guwahati Sightseeing & Departure
  • Visit Umananda Island & Assam State Museum.
  • Explore local Assamese markets.
  • Drop-off at Guwahati airport/railway station.
