Northeast India Tourism – A Perfect Gateway for Every Traveler

Northeast India is a hidden gem for honeymooners seeking romance, adventure lovers craving thrills, and budget travelers looking for offbeat yet affordable destinations. Whether you’re looking for snowy mountains, misty valleys, crystal-clear rivers, or vibrant cultural experiences, this region has something for everyone.

Best Honeymoon Destinations:
  • Gangtok & Tsomgo Lake (Sikkim): Breathtaking mountain views and serene monasteries
  • Shillong & Cherrapunji (Meghalaya): Romantic misty weather, waterfalls, and cozy cafes
  • Tawang & Ziro Valley (Arunachal Pradesh): Snow-capped peaks and lush green landscapes
  • Majuli Island (Assam): A peaceful river island with rich cultural experiences
  • Kaziranga, Pobitora & Manas (Assam): Thrilling jungle safaris with elephants and jeeps
Romantic Experiences:
  • Stay in a luxury cottage in Shillong with Umiam Lake views
  • Take a boat ride on Dawki River, known for its crystal-clear waters
  • Enjoy a romantic drive through Sela Pass, covered in snow during winter
  • Witness the breathtaking sunrise at Tawang Monastery
Best Adventure Destinations:
  • Dzukou Valley (Nagaland): A scenic trek to the “Valley of Flowers of the Northeast”
  • Siang River (Arunachal Pradesh): White-water rafting on one of India's wildest rivers
  • Kaziranga National Park (Assam): Jeep safari to spot the rare one-horned rhinoceros
  • Mawsmai & Siju Caves (Meghalaya): Caving experiences in limestone formations
Best Time to Visit:

Honeymoon & Leisure: March – June (Spring/Summer) or October – February (Winter for snowfall)
Adventure & Trekking: March – June (Best for treks & rafting), October – February (Snow adventures)

Northeast India Itinerary – 6 Days & 5 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Guwahati – Shillong
  • Arrive at Guwahati Airport/Railway Station
  • Drive to Shillong, en route visit Umiam Lake
  • Explore Police Bazaar and enjoy local cuisine
  • Overnight stay in Shillong
Day 2: Shillong – Cherrapunji Excursion
  • Visit Elephant Falls and Shillong Peak
  • Proceed to Cherrapunji, explore Nohkalikai Falls and Mawsmai Caves
  • Return to Shillong for an overnight stay
Day 3: Shillong – Kaziranga National Park
  • Drive to Kaziranga, home to the rare one-horned rhinoceros
  • Evening jungle safari in the national park
  • Overnight stay in Kaziranga
Day 4: Kaziranga – Dirang (Arunachal Pradesh)
  • Early morning elephant safari at Kaziranga
  • Drive to Dirang via scenic mountain roads
  • Visit Dirang Dzong and Hot Water Springs
  • Overnight stay in Dirang
Day 5: Dirang – Tawang
  • Travel to Tawang, en route visit Sela Pass and Jaswant Garh War Memorial
  • Explore Tawang Monastery, the largest in India
  • Overnight stay in Tawang
Day 6: Tawang – Guwahati Departure
  • Return to Guwahati via Bomdila
  • Visit Kamakhya Temple before departure
  • Trip ends with wonderful memories!
